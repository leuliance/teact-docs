<Callout type="info">
  Import **components** from `@teactjs/ui` and **everything else** (`createBot`, hooks, the router,
  `defineConfig`) from `@teactjs/core`. The old `@teactjs/react`, `@teactjs/runtime`, and
  `@teactjs/renderer` packages were folded into `@teactjs/core`.
</Callout>
